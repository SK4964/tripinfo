Kodaikanal, Tamil Nadu
Kodaikanal, often referred to as the Princess of Hill Stations, is a charming hill station located in the Palani Hills of the Western Ghats in Tamil Nadu. Established in 1845 by British bureaucrats, it has since become a popular retreat for those seeking respite from the heat of the plains. Kodaikanal is known for its lush greenery, mist-covered hills, and picturesque lakes.
Connectivity
  • Kodaikanal Bus Station, in Kodaikanal town.
  • Kodai Road Railway Station, about 80 KM away.
  • Madurai International Airport, about 115 KM away.
Best Time To Visit

The perfect time to experience Kodaikanal is from March to May and September to November. The weather is generally pleasant throughout the year.
