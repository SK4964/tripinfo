Sothavilai Beach

Sothavilai Beach or Chothavilai Beach is one of the longest natural sandy beaches in Tamil Nadu, stretching over 4 kilometers. Located in the Kanyakumari district, it's a serene and picturesque spot, perfect for a peaceful getaway

The beach is known for its golden sands and shallow waters, making it ideal for swimming and beach sports. It's a relatively clean and less crowded beach, offering a tranquil atmosphere.

While the beach is beautiful, visitors should be cautious of the strong waves, especially during high tide.
