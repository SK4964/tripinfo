Pykara Lake

Pykara Lake is a scenic spot located about 20 kilometers from Ooty in Tamil Nadu. The lake is formed by a dam constructed on the Pykara River, which originates from the Mukurthi peak.

The lake is a popular spot for boating, with options for motorboats and speedboats. It's a great way to enjoy the serene surroundings and the lush greenery.

The area around the lake is perfect for picnics, with a small cafeteria and restaurant available for visitors. It's a wonderful place to spend a day, whether you're looking to relax by the lake, enjoy a boat ride, or take a hike to the waterfalls.
